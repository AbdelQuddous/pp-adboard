AdBoard - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 29, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: AdBoard is designed with privacy-first principles. We only access the AdMob data you explicitly authorize and never share your information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 AdMob Data (With Your Permission)

When you grant authorization, we access the following AdMob data through Google's official API:

  • Account information (account ID, name, currency, timezone)
  • App information (app IDs, names, platforms)
  • Ad unit information (ad unit IDs, names, formats)
  • Revenue and performance metrics (earnings, impressions, clicks, eCPM, CTR)
  • Analytics data for specified date ranges

1.2 Google Account Information

We collect basic Google account information for authentication:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile picture (if available)
  • Google account ID

1.3 App Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:

  • App crashes and performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Device type and OS version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide AdBoard's services:

  • Display Analytics: Present your AdMob data in charts and reports
  • Authentication: Verify your identity and maintain secure sessions
  • Data Synchronization: Keep your analytics up-to-date
  • App Improvement: Enhance performance and fix issues
  • Caching: Store data locally for faster loading and offline access

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • AdMob data is cached locally on your device using secure storage
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely
  • Cache can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

3.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Google OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • No sensitive data stored on external servers
  • Regular security updates and improvements

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We DO NOT share your AdMob data with any third parties.

The only third-party service we interact with is:

  • Google Services: For authentication and AdMob API access
  • Firebase (if used): For crash reporting and analytics (anonymized)

5. Your Rights and Controls

5.1 Access Control

  • You can revoke AdMob access permissions at any time through your Google account settings
  • You can sign out of the app to stop data synchronization
  • You can delete cached data by clearing app data or uninstalling

5.2 Data Portability

  • Your AdMob data remains in your Google AdMob account
  • AdBoard only displays data you already own
  • No vendor lock-in - you can stop using AdBoard at any time

6. Demo Mode

When using demo mode:

  • Only sample/fake data is displayed
  • No real AdMob data is accessed
  • Demo sessions are not tracked or stored
  • Perfect for evaluating the app without data sharing

7. Children's Privacy

AdBoard is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where Google operates its services. All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and Google's security standards.

9. Data Retention

  • Cache Data: Stored locally until you clear it or uninstall the app
  • Authentication: Tokens expire automatically and are refreshed as needed
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data may be retained for app improvement purposes

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications (if applicable)
  • Updated effective date on this policy

11. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Store policies
  • Google API Services User Data Policy
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws
